Text Formatting


(b-tag) & (strong-tag) [Bold]

- (b-tag) makes the contents only bold.
- (strong-tag) makes the contents bold as well as gives the word priority over other word (for browser searches and readers)

b-tag : Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Fuga, adipisci?

strong-tag : Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Temporibus, eveniet!

(i-tag) & (em-tag) [Itallic]

- (i-tag) makes the contents only Itallic.
- (em-tag) makes the contents Itallic as well as gives the word priority over other word (for browser searches and readers)

i-tag : Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Fuga, adipisci?

em-tag : Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Temporibus, eveniet!

(small-tag) [Reduce font size]

- (small-tag) makes the content font size less than current.
small-tag : Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Fuga, adipisci?

(mark-tag) [Highlight text]

- (mark-tag) makes the content higlighted with a highlighter.
mark-tag : Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Fuga, adipisci?

(del-tag) [To specify something is Removed]

- (del-tag) makes the content striked out in middle , suggesting that this content is supposed to be deleted.
del-tag : Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Fuga, adipisci?

(ins-tag) [To specify something is Added]

- (ins-tag) makes the content underlined , suggesting that this content is supposed to be added in.
ins-tag : Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Fuga, adipisci?

(sub-tag) & (sup-tag) [Subscript & Superscript]

- (sub-tag) makes subscript appear at bottom.
- (sup-tag) makes superscript appear at top.

sub-tag : X 1 + X 2 + X 3

sup-tag : X 2 + Y 2 + Z 2
