(abbr-tag) [For abbriviations]

The full form will be displayed when hovered over the abbrivation
Example : WHO

(address-tag) [For specifying the browser about a physical location]

Address written normally inside a paragraph will be treated as normal text.
But address inside the address-tag will come n search results and other utilities.
Example :

Delhi, India

(cite-tag) [For specifying the main title of description ahead.]

(cite-tag) makes browser understand that the name inside (cite-tag) is the subject of lines following it.
Example : The Harry Pottter Series is written by J. K. Rowling. It has eight movies & an upcoming movie in 2022.
