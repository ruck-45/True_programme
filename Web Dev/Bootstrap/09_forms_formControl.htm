.form-control
      - It makes form elements (input,file,text,password,email,textarea etc..) more customized and modern
    





      .form-control-(size)
      - They control the size of the form control
      - They are separate classes and need to be used along with .form-control (.form-control .form-control-lg)
      - It can also work without .form-control (but we won't get the style)
      - They come in 3 sizes : 
        - form-control : default
        - form-control-sm : small
        - form-control-lg : large
    

      .form-label
      - customizes form label
    

      .form-text
      - customizes form texts (not input, texts written in from for info)
(default) Your password must be 8-20 characters long, contain letters and numbers, and must not contain spaces, special characters, or emoji.

(customized) Your password must be 8-20 characters long, contain letters and numbers, and must not contain spaces, special characters, or emoji. 

      .form-control-color
      - By default just by appplying form-control to input:color stretches the color window
      - We use .form-control-color to shrink it to its intended width
