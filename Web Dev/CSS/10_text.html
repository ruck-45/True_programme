  • white-space: (types of modes) ;
    • - it decides wheather to consider the whitespaces given in text or to override them.
    - we often see in html the extra space and line-brakes given is autometically ignored, 'white-space property' helps us customize those Properties
    - different modes include :
    • pre: preserves all spaces and line-brakes , & disables autometic word-wrap from browser side
    • nowrap: overrides all extra spaces and line brakes and also diable the autometic word-wrap from browser side i.e, whole paragraph is shown in one line
    - by default mode is normal.
    - Example :

    white-space: pre;
